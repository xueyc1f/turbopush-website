隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

感谢您使用本应用。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息，尤其是在通过 YouTube API 上传视频和使用 Google 帐号授权时。
Thank you for using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, especially when uploading videos via the YouTube API and using Google account authorization.

1. 我们收集的信息 / Information We Collect

  • Google 账户信息：仅在您授权本应用访问您的 YouTube 账户时获取，包括频道信息、视频上传权限等。
    Google account information: Obtained only when you authorize our application to access your YouTube account, including channel details and video upload permissions.
  • 视频信息：视频标题、描述、标签、分类和上传记录。
    Video information: Video title, description, tags, category, and upload records.
  • 应用使用数据：如 API 请求记录，用于调试和改进服务。
    Application usage data: API request logs used for debugging and service improvement.

2. 信息的使用方式 / How We Use Your Information

  • 上传视频至您的 YouTube 频道。
    Upload videos to your YouTube channel.
  • 管理视频的隐私状态、定时发布和封面设置。
    Manage video privacy status, scheduled publishing, and thumbnails.
  • 提供应用功能和用户支持。
    Provide application features and user support.

3. 信息共享与披露 / Information Sharing and Disclosure

我们不会将您的个人信息出售或出租给第三方。仅在以下情况下可能共享：
We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared in the following cases:

  • 遵守法律法规或法院、政府部门的要求。
    To comply with laws, regulations, or requests from courts or government authorities.
  • 与合作服务商进行服务执行，但仅限必要数据。
    With service partners for executing services, limited to necessary data only.

4. 信息存储与安全 / Data Storage and Security

  • 您的信息存储在安全的服务器上，并采取合理的技术和管理措施保护数据安全。
    Your information is stored on secure servers, with reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect it.
  • 我们使用 OAuth 2.0 token 进行授权，access token 会在程序运行时刷新，refresh token 安全存储，不会泄露给第三方。
    We use OAuth 2.0 tokens for authorization. Access tokens are refreshed during program execution, and refresh tokens are securely stored and not shared with third parties.

5. 用户权利 / Your Rights

  • 您可以随时撤销对本应用的 YouTube 访问授权。
    You can revoke access to your YouTube account by our application at any time.
  • 您可以请求我们删除存储的个人信息和上传记录。
    You can request deletion of stored personal information and upload records.
